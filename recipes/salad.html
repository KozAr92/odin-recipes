Chinese soupe salad

Delicious salad to make in 15 minutes. Perfect for every party. Suits everyone!

To make this salad you will need:

  • 3 pkgs of chinese sopu
  • 1 red paprika
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 can of sweet corn
  • 3 spoons of maionaise
  1. Boil water and prepare noodles
  2. Chop paprika and cucumber and add to noodles
  3. Add corn
  4. Add maionaise
  5. Blend everything carefully
  6. Enjoy!
